About Us

Parth Group

Parth Group is engaged with Housing Industry from past 25 years, making it the most trustworthy builders. We indulge ourselves in delivering aesthetically designed spaces and offering services in Bangalore, Mumbai and Vashi. Our advanced land enhancement focuses on developing elegant homes with the most precise guidelines and gracious environment.

To emphasize the latest trends, we are bringing up sophisticated homes for you to explore the supreme quality that are thoughtfully crafted and strategically located. Always a satisfied client equates to a happy home and it can only be achieved through absolute transparency and sincerity.
